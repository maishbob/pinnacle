# App Builder Prompt for Pinnacle Website

**Goal:** Create a high-fidelity, modern, and responsive corporate website for a technology solutions firm named **Pinnacle**.

**Brand Identity:**
- **Name:** Pinnacle
- **Tagline:** Innovative Tech Solutions
- **Vibe:** Professional, cutting-edge, trustworthy, global.
- **Target Audience:** Businesses seeking custom software, cloud migration, and digital transformation.

---

## Detailed Instructions

Create a single-page landing page with a sticky header and smooth scrolling navigation. The design should utilize plenty of whitespace, clear typography (Montserrat/Open Sans), and a professional color scheme (e.g., Deep Blue, White, and Slate Gray).

### File Structure
- **HTML5** for semantic structure.
- **CSS3** for styling (Flexbox/Grid).
- **JavaScript** for the mobile menu, testimonial slider, and form validation.

### Sections Required

1.  **Header / Navigation**
    - **Logo:** "Pinnacle" on the left.
    - **Menu:** Services, Process, Team, Contact.
    - **CTA Button:** "Contact Us" (distinct style).
    - **Mobile:** Hamburger menu that expands on click.

2.  **Hero Section**
    - **Headline:** "Transforming Ideas into Powerful Software Solutions"
    - **Subheadline:** "We build custom software that drives business growth and solves complex challenges for forward-thinking companies."
    - **Actions:** Two buttons: "Get Started" (Primary) and "Explore Services" (Secondary/Outline).
    - **Visual:** A high-quality tech/software illustration or background image on the right or as a background.

3.  **Clients / Trust Bar**
    - A banner displaying greyscale logos of trusted clients (e.g., St. Christopher, BE Energy, Zenith Tours) to establish authority.

4.  **Services Grid** (3 columns)
    - **Cards:** Each card must have an icon, title, description, and "Learn More" link.
    - **Service Items:**
        1.  **Custom Software Development:** Bespoke solutions for unique challenges.
        2.  **Mobile App Development:** Native and cross-platform apps.
        3.  **IT Support Services:** 24/7 technical support.
        4.  **Cloud Solutions:** Scalable infrastructure and migration.
        5.  **Data Analytics:** Business intelligence solutions.
        6.  **Cybersecurity:** Protection and compliance.

5.  **Process Timeline**
    - A step-by-step horizontal or vertical timeline showing the workflow:
    - **Steps:** 01 Discovery, 02 Planning, 03 Development, 04 Testing, 05 Deployment, 06 Maintenance.

6.  **Featured Projects** (Filterable Grid)
    - **Categories:** Web, Mobile, Cloud, AI.
    - **Project Cards:**
        - "Enterprise E-commerce Platform" (Web)
        - "Healthcare Communication Platform" (Mobile)
        - "Financial Services Migration" (Cloud)
        - "Predictive Analytics Dashboard" (AI)

7.  **Testimonials** (Carousel)
    - Slider showing client quotes.
    - **Content:**
        - "Pinnacle transformed our business..." - Sarah Johnson, CTO FinTech Innovations.
        - "Working with Pinnacle was a game-changer..." - Michael Torres, Product Manager.
    - Include navigation dots or arrows.

8.  **Team Section**
    - Clean profile cards with images, names, roles, and social icons (LinkedIn/Twitter).
    - **Members:**
        - Daniel Mutunga (CEO & Founder)
        - Gideon Nzoka (Senior Development Executive)
        - Peter Bosire (Operations Lead)

9.  **Contact Section**
    - **Left Col:** Contact Info (Email: info@pinnaclekenya.com, Phone: +254722527571, Business Hours).
    - **Right Col:** Contact Form (Name, Email, Comany, Phone, Service Dropdown, Message text area).

10. **Footer**
    - Logo, brief "About" text.
    - Links to Services and Support key pages.
    - Copyright © 2025 Pinnacle.

---

### Technical Constraints
- Ensure the site is fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop).
- Use semantic HTML tags (`<nav>`, `<section>`, `<article>`, `<footer>`).
- Ensure accessible contrast ratios and ARIA labels.
